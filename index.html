New Era of Evolution Web2

As you are aware, Web2 refers to the second generation of the World Wide Web, characterized by dynamic web pages, user interaction, and the emergence of social media platforms. Unlike its predecessor, Web1, which primarily consisted of static websites with limited user interaction, Web2 introduced a more interactive and collaborative online experience.

An Index of decentralization

Web3 refers to the next generation of the World Wide Web, which is characterized by decentralization, transparency, and trustlessness. Unlike Web2, where users rely on centralized platforms for services and interactions, Web3 aims to redistribute power and control back to the users through the use of blockchain technology and decentralized protocols.
